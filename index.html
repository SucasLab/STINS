Indoor Stability Experiments

Indoor Stability Experiments. Containing (a) Indoor Standing Test for privilege-trained quadrupedal locomotion, (b) Air-mattress traversal test for pure proprioception-trained quadrupedal locomotion and (c) Indoor Standing Test for Humanoid locomotion.

Outdoor Multi-terrain Traversal Experiments

Outdoor multi-terrain traversal experiment. The SLR variants are deployed to traverse an outdoor garden route in both forward and backward directions. The route contains flat ground, grass-covered soil slopes, slots, and stairs, with a total trajectory length of over 200m.
